About

Pampered Pooch has been expertly grooming Boulder's pets for over 20 years. We put care and compassion at the forefront of everything we do and we will ensure that your four-legged family member comes home clean and happy. All of our lovely groomers and faculty are certified independent contractors who work in accordance with the rules and regulations of PACFA. Pampered Pooch is a woman owned and operated small business.
